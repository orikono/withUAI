The new reality

Harm created in a moment can stay for a long time

In an era of widely available AI tools, a teen can find themselves inside an image they never took. The harm is real, even if the photo isn't — and it often enters the classroom before anyone has the chance to understand what happened.

WithU AI was built specifically for this moment. A place where talking can start, even when the words aren't yet in order.

Four principles

How WithU AI actually works

Every interaction begins with listening and moves to the next step — at your pace, never ahead of you.

Emotional intelligence

True listening, validation of feelings, no judgment. The first step is feeling heard — not solved.

Technical & legal support

Pointers to content-removal tools, help drafting an outreach to line 105, and a clear explanation of your rights — in plain human language.

A human-boundary model

Guiding questions — "Is this legal? Is this humiliating?" — that bring back ethical judgment, even under pressure.

Bridge to a caring adult

The bot doesn't replace a parent, teacher, or counselor — it helps you find the words to reach them.
