Tap "Start a conversation" on the home page, or "One step forward" in the menu. You will go through four short screens that help the bot understand how to walk with you — and then you enter the conversation itself.

Emergencies
Line 105 is open 24/7 for online harm to children and teens. If there is an immediate danger to life — call 100. The bot can stay with you alongside, but it is not a substitute for a phone call to a real person.
Line 105 is part of the Ministry of Health and Welfare, not the police. The conversation with them is discreet and is not an automatic report to the police. You can simply consult.
Every major platform has a reporting path for non-consensual images. The bot will walk you through the specific steps for each site and help you phrase the request — in plain language, without jargon.
Do not pay, do not respond, and do not delete evidence. The bot will walk with you step by step — documentation, reaching out to line 105, and choosing how and when to involve an adult. You do not have to handle this alone.
