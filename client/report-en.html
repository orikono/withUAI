Report

The first step is always the hardest. We are here so that you are not alone in it. At your own pace, with no unnecessary questions.

What happens when you press "Start"?

We will start with one short question — is this report about you, or about someone else. Based on your choice, the form will adapt and take you through a few short screens. Everything is anonymous, you can stop at any point, and we will not share any details with anyone without your permission.

Who is this report about?

Choose the right option. Then we will ask follow-up questions — at your own pace.

What is closest to what you are going through?

There is no right answer. Choose what feels closest — you can change it later.

When did it happen?

The timing helps the bot suggest suitable steps — but no need to be exact.

A tool for schools and educational institutions

A poster to print and hang at school

We have prepared a designed poster, ready to print in A4 size, that you can hang in hallways, classrooms, teachers' rooms, or on the notice board. The poster invites any teen experiencing harm, bullying, or online distress — to scan a QR code and receive immediate, anonymous, and safe help.

  • Built-in QR code Leads directly to our anonymous report form.
  • Ready to print in one click Designed A4 format, suitable for home or office printers.
  • Sensitive, non-judgmental language An inviting tone, speaking to teens at eye level.

Tip: in the print dialog, make sure the page size is set to A4 and that "print backgrounds" is enabled for the best result.
